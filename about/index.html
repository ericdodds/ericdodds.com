About Me

My name is Eric Dodds. I live in South Carolina.

I work in technology and play in the mountains. I’m passionate about strong coffee, following Christ, being a loving husband, and changing people’s lives through code education. Coffee helps with the last three.

I currently run a company called The Iron Yard, where my job ranges from writing to hiring to overall strategy. Our most recent adventure is building and expanding an code and design school. I’m humbled to work with two amazing business partners, Mason Stewart and Peter Barth.

Before my journey into the wild west of entrepreneurship, I worked as a marketing strategist and project manager for brands like Best Buy, Double Day, and Colonial Williamsburg.

I occasionally take on writing and branding projects under the moniker Shy Volcano. Let me know if you’re interested.

When I’m not at work you’re likely to find me mountain biking, rock climbing, tinkering in my shop, or delivering flowers for my wife’s business.
