New Hampshire

My wife and I recently went on an adventure in the Northeastern US. Our first stop was the Franconia Range in New Hampshire’s White Mountain National Forest). The terrain was simultaneously some of the most challenging and beautiful I’ve ever experienced. Fun fact: the highest surface wind speed observed by mankind (231mph) was recorded in 1934 on Mount Washington, the highest peak in both the White Mountains and the Northeastern US.
